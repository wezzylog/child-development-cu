About Cube

Beginning the Project we had a basic idea of what exactly we wanted: a toy to help with a child's development. Thus our methodology for the project formed: Continuous iteration while keeping child happiness in mind.

The Cube's purpose is not only to help with the development of a child's motor functions and hand-eye coordination, but also their reasoning and memory skills.

How Does This Work?

Touch & Light

When a child presses any of the 5 colored panels, it lights up and plays a sound describing the panel color.

Interactive Screen

The 6th side features an LCD touchscreen that functions as an infotainment system, enhancing basic functionality.

Multiple Games

ButtonMash (Simon Says), Mathematical games, TicTacToe, and Minesweeper for comprehensive development.

Game Levels

Level 1

The cube randomly lights up panels in sequence with sound. Child must correctly press the same sequence.

Level 2

Panels light up without audio and the cube no longer repeats the sequence.

Level 3

Pure audio-based level without visual cues - only playing panel sounds.

Level Infinity (Endless)

A combination mode that swaps between all three levels for ultimate challenge.

About Us

Revolutionizing Child Development Through Innovation

Welcome to Child Development Cube

Our device combines physical and digital interaction in a single unit that adapts to children's development from ages 3-10. The Cube's design evolved through iterative development, constrained by time and budget. We focused on creating engaging logic games using buttons and LEDs.

While initially conceived as a rotating piano-like structure, we ultimately chose a cubic design to enable more meaningful interaction, guided by children's toy design principles and safety considerations.

Frequently Asked Questions

What age group is the Child Development Cube designed for?

The cube is designed for children ages 3-10, with adaptive difficulty levels that grow with your child's development.

Is the cube safe for young children?

Yes, the cube is made with child-safe materials and has been tested for durability and safety. All components are non-toxic and designed to withstand regular use.

How long does the battery last?

The rechargeable battery provides approximately 6-8 hours of continuous play time and can be easily recharged using the included USB cable.

Can multiple children use the cube together?

Absolutely! The cube includes multiplayer games and collaborative activities that encourage social interaction and teamwork.
